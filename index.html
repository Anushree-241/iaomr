About the Convention

IAOMR is our national body representing oral medicine and radiology Specialists nation wide which conducts National Post Graduate convention every year. This is the first time to conduct such an event overseas on an international platform, Bangkok. Bangkok, Thailandâ€™s capital, is a large city known for ornate shrines and vibrant street life. Bangkok's real name is actually the longest city name in the world! In Thai, it's called Krung Thep Maha Nakhon. The city is known for cultural landmarks Buddhist temples and other tourist attractions. City has major Dental Institutions with Oral medicine and Radiology specializations The organizing team have chosen this place to provide scientific, cultural and social exposure of ths city to our specialty faculties and students.
Message from Organizing Chairman

Dear esteemed colleagues and Dental professionals We are thrilled to invite you to the Indian academy of oral medicine and radiology, post graduate convention 2025. This marks a historic occasion as we proudly present the first ever international PG convention dedicated to advancements the field of oral medicine and maxillofacial radiology at a post graduate level The convention promises to be a milestone event, featuring renowned international speakers from across the globe. This exceptional gathering will provide a unique platform for knowledge exchange, networking and collaboration among post graduate students, specialist and practitioners of oral medicine and radiology.
